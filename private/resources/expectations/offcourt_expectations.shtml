Off-Court Expectations

Academics

  • Attend all classes.
  • Do the work your teacher asks you to do.
  • Get at least a 3.0 GPA (won't be too hard if you do the first two things)
  • Your education and your ability to learn lasts a lifetime - take it seriously.

Legal

  • Don't get arrested.
  • No drinking or swearing while wearing spirit squad apparel.
  • No drinking within 24 hours of stunting or cheering.
  • No drugs - it's just a stupid thing to do.

Cheer/Fitness

  • Work out with weights and (if needed) cardio at least twice a week, preferably 3 or 4 times/week.
  • Stunt outside of practice at least once a week.

Email

  • Check your email at least every other day.
  • Respond immediately when asked to.

Overall

  • Be responsible - be honest with yourself and others, and if you say you'll do something, do it.
  • As a KU cheerleader, your actions represent not only yourself, but also the spirit squad (past, present, and future) and the university as a whole. Do us proud!
