Infractions and Consequences

Missed workout (according to trainer):

  • More than 1 unexcused absence per semester from mandatory strength and conditioning workouts will result in disciplinary action from coach/coordinator, up to or including dismissal from the team. Illness or special circumstances (prior approval required) are the only excuses for missing a conditioning session. Missing due to studying will not be excused.
  • 2 Unexcused, missed workouts will result in non-participation for a scheduled game coach chooses, and disciplinary action from coach up to or including dismissal for the team.

Late:

Team runs until you're late; It's an absence if > 10 min. late

Conduct detrimental to the team, including:

  • Inappropriate and/or disrespectful communication to teammates, coaches, or Athletic Dept.
  • 2Inappropriate social media posting or group text
  • 2Inability or unwillingness to do what is asked of you

1st: Written warning & individual conditioning
2nd: Benching
3rd: Dismissal

Missed deadlines

Individual conditioning; dismissal after 3rd offense.

Wrong attire

Individual conditioning; dismissal after 3rd offense.
