Uniforms

Your Responsibilities

  • Always fold your uniform! If you do, they won't wrinkle, and we're not going to cheer in wrinkled uniforms any more.
  • Don't lose your uniform or you'll be charged for it.
  • If you have a problem with a uniform, see Cat. She must approve any alterations BEFORE they are done (hopefully, you'll be able to switch with someone rather than alter it).
  • Turn in your uniforms quickly when you're asked to.

Bow Colors

Unless otherwise specified, the bow colors are:

New Blue or Diagonal uniform = White Bow
White uniform = Red Bow
Red/Blue uniform = Blue Bow
